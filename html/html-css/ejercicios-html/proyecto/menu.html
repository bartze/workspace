Menu on board

This is the primary menu that will be served aboard the Mars Express spacecraft during the duration of the mission. While it may not be as varied as the meals we enjoy on Earth, every meal has been carefully crafted to provide the astronauts with the essential nutrients they need to maintain optimal health and performance during their long journey to Mars. From rehydrated meals to specially formulated snacks, each item has been selected to withstand the rigors of space travel and to offer a taste of home.

Terraformed Vegetables

Savor the fruits of our Martian labor with this vibrant and flavorful dish. Our hydroponically grown vegetables, cultivated under the red Martian sun, are bursting with nutrients and taste. Experience the unique terroir of Mars in every bite.

Meat at martian atmosphere

Indulge in a truly out-of-this-world dining experience with our signature Martian meat. Grown and processed in our state-of-the-art facilities, this tender and juicy cut has been carefully cultivated to thrive in our planet's unique atmosphere. A taste sensation you won't find anywhere else.

Deeper algae cream

Embark on a celestial dessert adventure with our velvety Deeper Algae Cream. Harvested from the depths of our Martian oceans, this sweet and creamy treat is infused with the essence of our planet. A delightful and nutritious way to end your meal.
