Menu on board

This is the primary menu that will be served aboard the Mars Express spacecraft during the duration of the mission. While it may not be as varied as the meals we enjoy on Earth, every meal has been carefully crafted to provide the astronauts with the essential nutrients they need to maintain optimal health and performance during their long journey to Mars. From rehydrated meals to specially formulated snacks, each item has been selected to withstand the rigors of space travel and to offer a taste of home.
